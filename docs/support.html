Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bloom work?

Bloom tracks every glass of water you log and uses it to grow your virtual plant. The more consistently you drink water, the healthier and more vibrant your plant becomes. Missing days causes the plant to wilt — so staying hydrated is both fun and rewarding!

Is Bloom free to use?

Yes! Bloom's core features — water tracking, plant growth, achievements, and basic statistics — are completely free. Bloom Pro (advanced analytics, custom themes, and iCloud sync) is available as an optional upgrade.

How do I subscribe or purchase Bloom Pro?

Tap the "Upgrade Bloom Pro" button in the app's settings or profile page. Purchases are securely processed by Apple through the App Store.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Bloom and tap "Cancel Subscription". Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Can I use Bloom with Apple Health?

Yes! In Settings, tap "Connect Health App" to enable optional sync. Bloom can read and write water intake data to Apple Health.

How is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Optionally, you can enable iCloud sync to keep your data backed up and in sync across all your Apple devices.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Data Backup → Export. Your data will be saved as a JSON file that you can store in the Files app.

Does Bloom work offline?

Yes! Bloom works fully offline. All core features work without an internet connection.
