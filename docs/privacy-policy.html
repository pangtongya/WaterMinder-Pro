🌱 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Introduction

Bloom ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Bloom mobile application (the "App") and related services.

By using Bloom, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Health Data (Optional — Requires Your Permission)

With your explicit permission, Bloom can access Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read water intake data from Apple Health to track your hydration habits
  • Write water intake records to Apple Health for comprehensive health tracking

Important: We never transmit your health data to any third party. All health data remains on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

App Usage Data We Store Locally

  • Plant care records and growth history
  • Water intake logs (date, time, amount)
  • Achievement progress and unlock status
  • App settings and preferences
  • Custom themes and personalization choices

iCloud Sync Data (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We have no access to this data.

How We Use Your Information

  • Plant Growth: Your water intake data drives the virtual plant growth system
  • Statistics: We calculate drinking patterns and provide personalized insights
  • Reminders: Notification preferences enable us to send timely hydration reminders
  • iCloud Sync: Keeps your data consistent across all your Apple devices

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your iOS device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission. We do not maintain any external servers or databases containing your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Bloom uses the following Apple services (each governed by Apple's own privacy policy):

  • HealthKit: Optional reading/writing of health data (requires your explicit permission)
  • CloudKit: Optional encrypted backup and sync via your personal iCloud account
  • StoreKit 2: In-app purchase processing handled entirely by Apple

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell or rent your personal data to anyone
  • Share data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Use analytics services that collect personal information
  • Transfer your data to any third party, except as required by law

Your Rights

  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling Bloom from your device
  • Export your data in JSON format through the app's built-in backup feature
  • Disable or delete iCloud sync data at any time via iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

Bloom is rated 4+ (suitable for all ages) in the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
