🌱 Bloom Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Introduction

Bloom (水滴花园) is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Data We Collect

Health Data (Optional)

With your explicit permission, Bloom can:

  • Read water intake data from Apple Health to track your hydration habits
  • Write water intake records to Apple Health for complete health tracking

Important: We never share your health data with third parties. All health data stays on your device and in your iCloud account.

App Usage Data

  • Plant care records and growth history
  • Water intake logs
  • Achievement progress
  • App settings and preferences

How We Use Your Data

  • Plant Growth: Your water intake data drives the virtual plant growth system
  • Statistics: We calculate drinking patterns and provide insights
  • Reminders: Notification preferences help us send timely reminders
  • Sync: iCloud sync keeps your data consistent across devices

Data Storage

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device by default. We use iOS's secure storage mechanisms to protect your information.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. We cannot access this data.

Third-Party Services

Bloom uses the following Apple services:

  • HealthKit: For reading/writing health data (requires your permission)
  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud backup and sync
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases (processed by Apple)

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Use analytics services that collect personal information

Your Rights

  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • You can delete all app data by deleting the app
  • You can export your data through the app's backup feature
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

Children's Privacy

Bloom is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
